About

Seb is ¾ English and ¼ Finnish.
As an ex-St Paul's Cathedral chorister, he has effectively been singing professionally since he was seven. Today, he's studying Contemporary Music at Berklee College of Music in Boston. Motown veteran Bobby Taylor describes him as ‘probably the third best singer I taught’ – the second best was Michael Jackson; and the best was Marvin Gaye.
